The Market

Why families choose West Hills.

West Hills is a name that carries weight in the West San Fernando Valley. Carved out of Canoga Park in 1987 and quietly built into one of the area's most sought-after residential communities, the 91307 zip code draws families with a simple, powerful combination: strong schools, generous lots, upscale homes, and a pace of life that feels removed from the city even while staying fully connected to it.

The anchor is El Camino Real Charter High School — nationally renowned for academics, athletics, and its powerhouse Academic Decathlon program. For families with children, school access is often the deciding factor that brings them to West Hills over comparable West Valley options. The neighborhood rewards that decision with well-maintained streets, mature landscaping, and a strong sense of community.

For buyers, West Hills also represents smart positioning: you get the Calabasas feel and proximity at a more attainable price point. Borders Hidden Hills to the west, Calabasas just beyond — the upside trajectory here is well established.

Neighborhoods

Where to live in West Hills.

West Hills is a tight-knit community with distinct pockets. Here's how I help buyers think about each one.

Valley Circle & The Hills

The prestige corridor of West Hills — hillside homes, sweeping views, larger lots, and the quiet that comes with elevation. Buyers willing to climb the hill are rewarded with the best views and the strongest long-term appreciation.

El Camino Real School Zone

Homes feeding into ECRCHS are the most actively searched in the neighborhood. Demand stays consistent year-round — families specifically target this pocket, which supports values even in softer markets.

Shadow Ranch Area

A community-minded pocket anchored by Shadow Ranch Park — with its equestrian center, rose garden, and mature oaks. Walkable, established, and beloved by long-term residents who value character over flash.

Bell Canyon Adjacent

The southwestern edge of West Hills borders the entrance to Bell Canyon, offering a canyon-adjacent feel with larger parcels and more privacy than central West Hills neighborhoods.

West Hills Pointe & Gated Enclaves

Newer gated communities offering modern floor plans, HOA maintenance, and a more polished entry experience — popular with move-up buyers who want the newest product in the area.

Vanowen & Sherman Corridor

The established, well-priced backbone of West Hills — single-family homes on regular lots close to shopping, commuter routes, and services. Strong value entry point into the 91307.
