### 12. Prop 19 for long-held Chatsworth homes
The longer your family has held in 91311, the wider the gap between your Prop 13 tax bill and today’s value.

Since 2021, that low base only survives inheritance if a child moves in as their primary residence. Rent it out instead and reassessment can multiply the bill. Plan before the transfer — not after.
