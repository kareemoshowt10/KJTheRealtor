Find Amal in Chatsworth.

Everything you need to plan the visit lives here now: the address, the hours, the call-first booking path, and a clearer sense of what Amal's Salon offers.

How long has Amal's Salon been here?
Amal's Salon has been serving the Chatsworth community since 1985. That kind of longevity does not happen by accident. It comes from trust, repeat relationships, and a lifelong dedication to community values.
What does the salon offer?
Amal's Salon offers signature cuts, custom color and highlights, editorial styling, blowouts, hair and scalp rituals, extensions, refined grooming, beauty add-ons, and custom bridesmaid or private group pamper experiences.
What is the atmosphere like?
The atmosphere is attentive, direct, professional, and personal. Amal leads with faith, treats people like family, and speaks in a way that is clear rather than polished for show.
When can I come in?
Regular hours are Tuesday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Saturday from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM, and Sunday plus Monday closed. Special events or parties may be accommodated earlier or later by request.
Do you begin with a consultation?
Yes. For color, corrective work, extensions, luxury upgrades, or a fuller image shift, the best place to begin is with a conversation about goals, current condition, and how much maintenance you actually want to live with. New guests can also ask whether any first-visit courtesy is currently available.
