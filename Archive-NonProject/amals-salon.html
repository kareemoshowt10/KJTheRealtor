How long has Amal's Salon been here?
Amal's Salon has been serving the area since 1981. That kind of longevity does not happen by accident. It comes from trust, repeat relationships, and work people stay loyal to.

Amal's Salon offers women's and men's haircuts, coloring, highlights, hairstyling, blowouts, hair treatments, perms, hair extensions, beard and mustache work, manicure and pedicure, and eyelash services.

The atmosphere is attentive, direct, professional, and personal. Amal leads with faith, treats people like family, and speaks in a way that is clear rather than polished for show.
