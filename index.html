The Method

The Generational Wealth Method™

Anyone can open a door. This is a deliberate system around the largest asset your family will ever own — so each move compounds into the next.

No. 01

Position

Map where your family is today and where it should be in ten to twenty years — before a single showing. Every move serves the bigger picture.

No. 02

Protect

Disciplined pricing, negotiation, and terms defend your equity on both sides of the table. The goal is never just to close — it's to close without leaving value behind.

No. 03

Compound

Timing, leverage, and tax-aware strategy — 1031 exchanges, Prop 19 transfers, capital-gains exclusions — turn one transaction into decades of growth.

No. 04

Transfer

The move most agents never discuss: structuring ownership so the wealth you build survives the handoff to the next generation intact.
