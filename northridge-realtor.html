The Market

Why buyers discover Northridge.

Northridge is the central San Fernando Valley's steady, practical, and increasingly well-positioned market. It doesn't have the flash of Calabasas or the premium of Woodland Hills — what it has is space, value, and established character in a location that's hard to beat for connectivity. The 405 and 118 freeway corridors, CSUN, major medical centers, and the Valley's commercial backbone all run through or adjacent to Northridge.

For buyers who want larger lots, single-family homes, and real square footage per dollar, Northridge consistently delivers. The pockets range from the upscale hillside of Northridge Knolls — with views, larger parcels, and a quiet elevation above the Valley floor — to the tree-lined streets of Sherwood Forest and the student-adjacent areas near California State University, Northridge that attract investors and house-hackers.

For families, investors, and heirs navigating inherited properties, Northridge represents a significant and increasingly recognized opportunity. Many homes here were purchased decades ago by long-term owners, creating a steady pipeline of estate and trust sales with substantial embedded equity.

Inherited & Probate Homes · Northridge

Selling an inherited home in Northridge.

Step-up in basis, clean-out coordination, and LA County probate guidance — calm help when your family needs it most.

Northridge has one of the largest concentrations of long-term homeowners in the San Fernando Valley. Many properties here were purchased in the 1970s, 1980s, and 1990s — which means inherited homes in Northridge often carry decades of appreciation and complex family situations. Understanding the financial and legal landscape before listing is essential.

The most powerful benefit available to heirs is the step-up in basis. When you inherit a home, your tax basis for capital gains purposes resets to the fair market value at the date of death — not what the original owner paid years or decades ago. A Northridge home purchased for $120,000 in 1985 and now worth $850,000+ means heirs could sell with little or no capital gains tax owed. That's potentially hundreds of thousands of dollars in tax savings — a benefit many families unknowingly forfeit by moving too quickly without the right advice.

Northridge is in LA County, so probate proceedings run through LA County Superior Court — the Stanley Mosk Courthouse in downtown Los Angeles. Whether the estate is in full probate or held in a trust, each path has specific steps, timelines, and documentation. I work alongside estate attorneys, coordinate fair-market pricing, connect families with trusted clean-out professionals, and manage the listing and sale — so the process moves forward clearly, without overwhelming the family.

Step-Up in Basis

Inheriting a Northridge home often resets the tax basis to current fair market value — eliminating capital gains on decades of appreciation. Confirm your specific situation with a CPA or estate attorney before any sale decision. I can refer you to professionals who understand LA County estate situations.

Long-held family homes typically need full estate clean-outs before reaching market. I work with estate sale coordinators, cleanout services, and donation organizations throughout Northridge and the central Valley — so the home is market-ready without the family managing every task alone.
